Frequently Asked Questions


What schools does Graderoom Support?

Graderoom currently only supports Bellarmine College Preparatory, BASIS Independent Silicon Valley, and Notre Dame High School.



Which version of Graderoom is supported?

We currently only support Graderoom's Beta Build.



Where can I find my pairing key?

It's located at https://beta.graderoom.me/. It will generate a key that expires in an hour. Generate a key and place it in the box above.



It's not letting me Generate a Pairing Key.

You need at least one sync to be able to generate a key. This verifies you are a real person.
